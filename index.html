The church problem

  • Most churches are corrupt as they no longer present God in full scriptural truth.
  • God never called for a church of thousands of denominations with different doctrines and theologies. He is not the author of confusion.

  • The church offers you a view of God through their "lens". At best, that lens is partially covered, cloudy, and dirty. Remove that lens and you can more clearly see God.
